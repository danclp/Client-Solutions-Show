Tech Tips

  • The webinar is being streamed through your computer. Ensure that your computer speakers (or headset) are turned on. For the best stream quality, please make sure your browsers are updated to the latest version. We recommend joining our webinar via Google Chrome on your desktop/laptop.
  • If you are experiencing a disruption in network connection or a lag in streaming, push F5 on your keyboard to refresh your page.
  • Please close any programs or browser sessions running in the background that could cause issues. Webinars are bandwidth intensive, so closing any unnecessary brower tabs will help conserve your bandwidth.
